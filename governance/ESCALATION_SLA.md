# Escalation SLA

## Priority
- P1: acknowledge <= 15 min, owner assignment <= 30 min
- P2: acknowledge <= 2 h, owner assignment <= 4 h
- P3: acknowledge <= 1 business day

## Trigger conditions
- Gate blocked > SLA
- Security/compliance uncertainty
- Cross-functional dependency unresolved
